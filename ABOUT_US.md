# About Us: VYBZ App

VYBZ App, a brainchild of VYBZ Inc., brings to you a unique way to forge real-world connections at your favourite local spots. Whether you're in your dorm, class, or at a local hangout, VYBZ makes it a breeze to discover and engage with people around you. It’s the pioneer in offering a true location-based social experience. With VYBZ, exploring who's around, messaging them, and igniting real conversations with real people in your immediate surroundings has never been more exciting.

## Key Features

*   **Real-Time Connection:** Dive into two dynamic feeds populated with content from users nearby: the Neighbourhood Mode and the Around Me Mode.
*   **Neighbourhood Mode:** Get a glimpse of what's buzzing in your town and jump into live chat to interact with other users instantly.
*   **Around Me Mode:** Discover what’s happening around you and join live group chats to meet people within a shout away.
*   **Augmented Reality Fun:** Link your Bitmoji and immerse yourself in fun with augmented reality through Snap Lenses powered by Snap Inc.’s Camera Kit. These lenses morph as per your location on VYBZ, letting your creative side soar.
*   **Direct Messaging & Friendship:** Befriend other users, slide into direct messages, and keep the conversation going.
